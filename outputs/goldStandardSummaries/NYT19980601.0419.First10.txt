BOEING-CONTRIBUTE (Washington) _ Boeing, the nation's second largest defense contractor, has vaulted into first place in a category that really matters in Washington: campaign contributions.
The company has poured more than $1 million into federal campaign coffers over the past 17 months, according to a study released yesterday.
The report comes as Boeing is anxiously awaiting the upcoming debate over Most Favored Nation trade status for China, which begins Wednesday and is expected to be ugly.
