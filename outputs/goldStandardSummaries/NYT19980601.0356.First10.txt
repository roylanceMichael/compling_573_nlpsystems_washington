DELTA-BRAZIL _ Delta Air Lines is being asked to buy a chunk of Transbrasil, its marketing partner in Brazil.
Transbrasil apparently needs cash amid fare wars that followed deregulation of Brazil's industry.
Delta, which bought 35 percent of AeroPeru in March, declined comment but acknowledged its code-share partnership with Transbrasil is important to its own service.
