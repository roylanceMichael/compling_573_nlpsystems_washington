PARIS _ With less than two weeks to go before the soccer World Cup kicks off in Paris on June 10, France has got the jitters.
Two weeks earlier, an unexploded bomb had been found in front of France Telecom's offices in Paris's northeast.
The Chippendales, a male striptease quintet, are putting on a show in Paris from June 15 entitled ``Les filles sans foot,'' which means ``Girls without football'' but also sounds like ``Les filles s'en foutent'' _ ``Girls don't give a damn'' (or something rather ruder).
And two French train-drivers' unions have called for a 24-hour (repeatable) stoppage on June 10, the day of the World Cup kick-off.
